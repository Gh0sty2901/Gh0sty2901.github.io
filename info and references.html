Ability Scaling

In league of legends, Champions scale depending on their level and skill points put into their abilities. In the table below shows Veigar's abilities and how they scale throughout the game. As you will notice he scales off of Ability power and with more ability power he deals way more damage with his abilities. His cooldowns also reduce for every skill point invested into each ability.
